About Us

Recognizing that the sharing, public dissemination, and analysis of mass spectrometry data has become a major challenge for many programs, Stratus Biosciences was founded with the ambitious goal to solve these problems. Stratus Biosciences is a not-for-profit company based in Seattle, WA with the goal of building and providing professionally developed software solutions for the mass spectrometry community. The Chorus Project is our cloud based platform for providing these services.
